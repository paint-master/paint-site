Exterior Painting FAQs

How long does exterior painting usually take?

Most residential repaints take 3–5 days depending on size, prep needs, and weather. We give clear timelines upfront and stay on schedule.

Do you pressure wash every exterior before painting?

Absolutely. Every project starts with a full pressure wash to remove dirt, mildew, and loose paint — essential for proper adhesion and lasting results.

What kind of paint do you use?

We primarily use Sherwin-Williams exterior coatings, designed for maximum durability against Alabama’s sun, humidity, and storms.

Do you handle surface repairs before painting?

Yes. We repair damaged trim, siding, fascia, and wood rot before any primer or paint is applied. Prep is everything.

Is your work guaranteed?

100%. Every exterior project is backed by a workmanship guarantee. We don’t leave until you’re fully satisfied.
