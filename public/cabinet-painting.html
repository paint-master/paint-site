Cabinet Painting Like You’ve Never Seen

Surface Prep That Sticks

Cabinets are cleaned, degreased, and sanded with precision. Every imperfection is filled, caulked, and leveled. This foundation ensures proper adhesion and flawless results. It’s the gritty work done right.

Shop Spraying & Curing

We carefully remove your cabinet doors and drawers and transport them to our dedicated spray shop. Here, in a controlled, dust-free environment, they receive multiple coats of high-end primer and a professional-grade topcoat, ensuring a durable, glass-smooth finish with zero brush marks.

On-Site Spray Zone & Prep

For the cabinet frames and fixed elements in your home, we meticulously mask off surrounding areas to create a sealed, dust-free spray zone. This military-precision masking ensures no overspray or mess, maintaining a clean environment while we achieve the same factory-quality finish on site.
