Frequently Asked Questions

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Dependable Painting is fully licensed and insured in Alabama, including general liability and workers’ comp coverage for your peace of mind.

Do you offer free estimates?

Absolutely. You can request a free estimate online or by phone. We provide detailed, no-obligation quotes for all projects.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Baldwin and Mobile Counties, including Fairhope, Daphne, Spanish Fort, Robertsdale, and Bay Minette. We generally do not service Gulf Shores or Orange Beach.

Who performs the work?

All work is performed by our dedicated in-house crew, led by the owners. We never use subcontractors, ensuring consistent quality and accountability.
